Let's Start Learning!

1.Hiring additional outside third-party companies is beneficial:

Managers need to hire some professional help to get their customers acquainted
with the things that they need to be prepared for before joining in on one of the
tours that Ghost Tours offers. Managers are not an expert on everything, and some outside
insights will be best opportunity to help increase the business and improve their ratings with customers.
They need to satisfy all their customers, including the unhappy ones. According to the Mind Tools Content Team,
once you hear that your customers are unhappy, you adjust your mindset, get into the customer service mindset,
and solve the issue or issues that your customer is having. When talking to the customer about their problem or problems,
repeat their concerns back to them as you are actively listening to what they are saying and present them with a solution to the problem.
Managers are to immediately act on the problem that occurred. Once they have fixed the problem with the customer,
they will need to do a follow-up with them and make sure that their needs are met.

2.How to handle work arguments between employees:

One of the severe potential consequences of not handling the situation
appropriately is customers overhearing the entire fight between the Owner
and their partner with the desk attendant. This could cause customers to not
want to come back the next time because of how the attendant did not handle
the issue appropriately. According to Erin Hueffner with Zendesk, when approaching
a customer situation where an argument has occurred, you should approach the situation
with empathy if other people could not want to intervene into the situation and make it worse.
Customers could start leaving when the argument is fully heard by everyone which will cause
loss of business and loss of profits. Customers could start to be so outraged by the situation
and not come back again and write bad reviews of the company which will cause even more loss
in profits and loss of potential future customers and business. This situation has
differently derailed customer satisfaction expectations and will cause a lot of
controversies about how the business runs, causing more loss of business and so on.
In all, a lot of loss of profits, business, and customer satisfaction could be lost
if the situation is not handled correctly right away.

3. How Managers should approach their employees with things that they need to do to improve profits:

Managers are doing what they are supposed to be doing as a manager but could approach the employee a
little differently about the situation. The manager should tell the employee exactly what they is doing
wrong and tell her how she could fix it. According to Indeed for employers, employers should keep a
positive perspective when talking to their employees, keep a record of their bad behaviors, and
remain professional when talking with their employees. I would recommend to the manager that they
follow these steps that Indeed recommends. Yes, the employee will still behave the way that she is,
but at least he will have a record of it and the potential to fire her if it needs to be. The manager
should remain calm and professional when telling the employee that their tips sales are low. These
are the things I recommend to managers do to keep themselves in an acceptable position with all their employees.

4. How to handle reservations at restaurants:

Reservations are something that can be a huge hassle for restaurants to handle so they do not offer them,
but those that do should have solutions in place to help guide restaurants people capacity. According to TouchBistro,
restaurants can place time limits on reserved tables, do not reserve all your tables and implement On-Waiting-List reservations.
I believe that the best solution to this issue is to have all restaurants except reservations, but limit how many
they take in a day and limit the amount of people that can be in a reservation. Another solution would be to not
to except big parties of people for tables and only expect parties of like 12-15 people max. This will help
restaurants manage the amount of people that can be seated at a time and make things be an easier flow in the
restaurants for employees to manage. Another solution would be to limit the number of people that are waiting
in the waiting area to be seated so that the restaurants do not get overcrowded and start pissing off
customers and employees. All these things will help ensure that hosts can get a better grip off how
to manage reservations at their restaurants and keep customers from getting to pissed off for not being
able to be seated when they want to be seated.
